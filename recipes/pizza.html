Pizza

What is Pizza?

Pizza is a very popular Italian cheesy bread. Very yummy!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes - I love using cherry or grape tomatoes here for an extra sweet flavor.

  • Red Onions - Red adds nice color here, and their flavor makes them stand out above other onion varieties.

  • Dough - a must! ðŸ™‚

Directions

  1. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough.

  2. Top with the fresh mozzarella and tomatoes and bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned.

  3. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes.

  4. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
